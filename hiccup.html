Hiccup Horrendous Haddock III

Hiccup Horrendous Haddock the Third is the main protangist in the How to Train You Dragon series, appearing in all the movies and TV shows. He is the son of Stoick of Vast, later becoming the Chief of Berk. as well as the son of Valka.

He is 15 the first film, How to Train Your Dragon, as well as the TV shows, Dragons: Riders of Berk and Defenders of Berk, about 18-19 in the Netflix show, Race to the Edge, 20 in the second film, How to Train Your Dragon 2 and 21 in the third and final film, How to Train Your Dragon: The Hidden World.

Hiccup's Story

Hiccup was born early to Stoick the Vast and Valka. Because of this, he was given the name Hiccup. But when he was still a baby, Valka was kidnapped by a Stormcutter during a dragon attack, never to be seen again.

At the beginning of the first movie, Hiccup was seen as an embassassment, both to the tribe of Berk and to his father. He was a runt and not pyhsical strong compared to other Vikings. However, he became very good at inventing things due to being Gobber's blacksmith apprentice. So such so, that during a regular dragon attack, he used one of his inventions and managed to shoot down a Night Fury, one of the most feared dragons. But doing this caused more chaos and no one believed him, so he let on his own to find the Night Fury. However, he wasn't able to kill him, so he lets him go.
