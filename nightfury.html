Night Fury

The Night Fury is a medium-sized Dragon, that is part of the Strike Class. It's first appeared in the franchise the first movie, How to Train Your Dragon.

Appearance

Egg

A Night Fury egg has an oblong shape, similar to a chicken egg, and smooth. They are jet black to blend in to the night sky.

Hatchling to Adult

They are medium-sized with a sleek, dark body with dark scales. They also have lighter patterns that resemble the ones on a manta ray.

A Night Fury have two pairs of wings, two mobile, ear-like appendages on the back of its head that show its mood, as well as help it hear things. They have a pair of large, yellowish-green eyes and sharp retractable teeth. They also have a short neck and lacks any kind of horns, which is very different from most dragon species.

Titan Wing

It is unknown what a Titan Wing Night Fury looks like because Grimmel the Grisly hunter them down to near extinction. The only known Night Fury, Toothless, has not yet reached this stage.

Abilities

Firepower

The Night Fury has the ability to shoot bursts of purple-coloured flmaes, which are composed of acetylene and oxygen. They have control of their fire, from a mild-warning shot to a full-power explosive blast. This type of blast has been refer to as a plasma blast, first by Hiccup.

Jaws

The Night Fury have incredible jaw strength. They also have the ability to retract their their teeth, to avoid chipping and breaking when not in use.

Known Dragons

Toothless

Toothless is the only known Night Fury in the series, making his debut appearance in the first film. He is Hiccup's dragon and best friend. He has the general appearnce of a Night Fury, with some differences. He doesn't have a left tailfin, which he lost due to Hiccup. It is replaced with a mechanical fin which Hiccup constructs. He also donnes a rather large scar on his right shoulder/neck.
